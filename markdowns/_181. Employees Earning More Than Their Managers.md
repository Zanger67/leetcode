### SQL
#### [e181.sql](<../my-submissions/e181.sql>)
```SQL
SELECT 
    e.name AS 'Employee'
FROM
    Employee e JOIN Employee m ON e.managerId = m.id
WHERE
    e.salary > m.salary;
```
