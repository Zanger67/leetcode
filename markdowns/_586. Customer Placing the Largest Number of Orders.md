### SQL
#### [e586.sql](<../my-submissions/e586.sql>)
```SQL
SELECT
    o.customer_number
FROM 
    Orders o
GROUP BY
    customer_number
ORDER BY
    COUNT(*) DESC
LIMIT 1;
```
