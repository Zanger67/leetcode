#### [e197.sql](<../my-submissions/e197.sql>)
```SQL
SELECT 
    w1.id
FROM 
    Weather w1 JOIN Weather w2 ON DATEDIFF(w1.recordDate, w2.recordDate) = 1
WHERE 
    w1.temperature > w2.temperature;
```
